# Tiny Wins Society - Health Points Game

## Scoring System

### Daily Points (Max 65 pts/day)

#### Health Basics
- B12 supplement -- 5 pts
- Iron tablet -- 5 pts
- Pumpkin seeds -- 5 pts
- Fiber ≥ 25g -- 10 pts
- Water ≥ 2L -- 10 pts
- Sleep ≥ 9 hours -- 15 pts
- Calorie deficit -- 15 pts

#### Movement (NEW!)
- Yoga class (15 min walk each way) = 10 pts
- 30 min walk = 10 pts
- 45 min workout = 15 pts
- 1 hr active movement = 20 pts
- Gentle stretching/recovery day = 5 pts

### Streak Bonuses
- 3 days: +10
- 7 days: +25
- 14 days: +50
- 21 days: +75
- 30 days: +150

### Rewards
- 500 pts = $10 clothing reward

## Rules
- Movement must be intentional (not just daily steps)
- Can be verified by:
  * Heart rate tracking
  * Workout log
  * Self-reporting
- Encourages consistent, varied movement
- Aligns with fitness goals (yoga, half marathon training)
