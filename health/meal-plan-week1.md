## Meal Prep Tips (Sunday Evening Batch)
- Soak lentils/dal for Monday-Tuesday
- Cook a pot of brown rice (stores 3-4 days)
- Wash and chop vegetables for the week
- Make coconut chutney (lasts 3-4 days refrigerated)
- Prepare overnight oats for Tuesday
- Roast makhana and chana for snacks
- Make trail mix portions

## Nutrient Highlights Per Day (Approximate)
- **Protein:** 55-70g (from dal, paneer, yogurt, tofu, nuts, seeds, legumes)
- **Iron:** 18-25mg plant-based (enhanced with vitamin C from lemon/citrus)
- **Omega-3:** 2-3g ALA (flax, chia, walnuts) + supplement for DHA/EPA
- **Fiber:** 30-40g (whole grains, legumes, vegetables)
- **Zinc:** 8-12mg (pumpkin seeds, chickpeas, lentils)
- **Selenium:** 55-100mcg (Brazil nuts)

## Anti-Bloating Notes
- All lentils/legumes: soak minimum 6-8 hours, cook with cumin + ajwain
- Moong dal is lightest on digestion -- use it more on high-bloat days
- Add fennel seeds to cooking or drink fennel tea after meals
- Avoid raw salads at dinner (cook vegetables instead)
- Buttermilk with lunch aids digestion (cumin, salt, curry leaves)
