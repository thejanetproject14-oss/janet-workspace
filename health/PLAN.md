## Supplement Stack (discuss with doctor before starting)

| Supplement | Dose | When | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Iron bisglycinate | 25-30mg | Morning, with vitamin C source | Iron deficiency (gentle form, less GI upset) |
| B12 methylcobalamin | 1000-2000mcg sublingual | Morning | B12 deficiency (plant-based diet) |
| Magnesium glycinate | 200-400mg | Before bed | Cortisol, sleep onset, inflammation |

| Vitamin D3 + K2 | 2000 IU D3 + 100mcg K2 | With food | Thyroid support, immunity (SG indoor lifestyle) |
| Zinc picolinate | 15-25mg | With dinner | Thyroid, immunity, skin |
| Selenium | 2-3 Brazil nuts/day OR 100mcg supplement | Morning | Thyroid (converts T4 to T3) |
