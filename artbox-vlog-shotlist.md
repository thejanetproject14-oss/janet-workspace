**Style:** Vlog/BTS, raw energy, Nirmal Pillai vibes
**Length:** 60-90 sec
**Format:** 9:16 (Reels/TikTok)
**Shoot across:** Day 2 (Sat) + Day 3 (Sun)

### 2. THE BOOTH
- [ ] Wide shot of your full booth setup — hero shot, get this clean
- [ ] Close-up product display — Lip Ganache lined up, charms hanging

### 3. THE ACTION
- [ ] Someone spinning the wheel — film their reaction

- [ ] You talking to a customer (from the side, candid — don't stage it)

- [ ] Checking your phone/sales — candid
- [ ] End of day — packing up, tired face, real

### 6. THE CLOSER (deals segment)

## FILMING TIPS
- **Use DJI Pocket 3** for walking/BTS shots (stabilised)
- **Use phone** for selfie/talking to camera
- Shoot VERTICAL (9:16) for everything
- Get minimum 2-3 sec per shot (gives you edit room)
- Don't overthink — messy = authentic for this format
- Film the closer segment (deals) when the booth is quiet, good lighting

## EDIT STRUCTURE
```
0:00-0:03  Hook text + arrival shot
0:03-0:15  Setup montage (fast cuts, music)
0:15-0:35  The action: customers, spins, swatches, sales
0:35-0:45  Tired founder moment (real, funny)
0:45-1:00  Closer: you to camera, show deals, booth number
1:00-1:05  End card: @analemma.shop + Booth 185
```

## AUDIO
- Trending audio OR soft background music for the montage
- Your voice for the hook + closer (film separately if booth is noisy)
- Option: voiceover the whole thing in post
