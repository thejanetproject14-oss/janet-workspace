# 💰 Pre-Fundraise Checklist: Path to $8M Series A

**Timeline:** 12-15 months to Series A | **Angel Target:** $200-500K (Month 6-9) | **Series A Target:** $8M SGD (Month 12-15)

---

## 1. 📋 Legal & Corporate Structure

### ACRA & Corporate Housekeeping
| Task | When | Cost (SGD) | Priority | Notes |
|------|------|------------|----------|--------|
| **Update ACRA shareholding structure** | Month 1 | $50-150 | Must-have | Formalize founder equity allocation, prepare for ESOP |
| **Draft shareholder agreement** | Month 2-3 | $3,000-5,000 | Must-have | Include vesting schedules, drag-along, tag-along rights |
| **Establish Employee Stock Option Pool (ESOP)** | Month 4-5 | $2,000-4,000 | Must-have | Typical 10-20% pool for Series A readiness |
| **Director's resolution for fundraising** | Month 5 | $500-1,000 | Must-have | Board authorization to issue new shares |
| **Update constitutional documents** | Month 3-4 | $2,000-3,000 | Must-have | Ensure investor-friendly articles of association |

### IP & Trade Secrets
| Task | When | Cost (SGD) | Priority | Notes |
|------|------|------------|----------|--------|
| **File provisional patent applications** | Month 2-3 | $1,500-3,000 each | Nice-to-have | For unique formulations/processes |
| **Employment agreements with IP clauses** | Month 1 | $1,000-2,000 | Must-have | All employees must assign IP to company |
| **Non-disclosure agreements (NDAs)** | Month 1 | $500 | Must-have | Standard templates for investors, suppliers |

**Singapore-specific notes:**
- Use ACRA BizFile+ for all corporate filings
- Consider Enterprise Singapore's Startup SG scheme for legal cost grants
- File trademarks in Class 3 (cosmetics) and Class 35 (retail services)

## 3. 📊 Business Metrics to Hit

### Revenue Targets
| Metric | Month 6 (Angel) | Month 12 (Series A) | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|---------------------|--------|
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue** | $15K-25K | $80K-120K | Focus on subscription/repeat customers |
| **Annual Revenue Run Rate** | $200K-300K | $1M-1.5M | Based on monthly performance |
| **Revenue Growth Rate** | 15-25% MoM | 10-20% MoM | Consistent month-over-month growth |

### Unit Economics (Must Track)
| Task | When | Priority | Target Metrics |
|------|------|----------|---------------|
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | Month 2 | Must-have | <$25 for organic, <$50 for paid |
| **Lifetime Value (LTV)** | Month 3 | Must-have | >$150 (3x LTV:CAC ratio minimum) |
| **Gross Margin Analysis** | Month 1 | Must-have | Maintain 85%+ margins per product |
| **Monthly cohort retention** | Month 4 | Must-have | >60% Month 1, >40% Month 3 |

### Operational Metrics
| Task | When | Priority | Notes |
|------|------|----------|--------|
| **Inventory turnover** | Monthly | Must-have | Target 8-12x annually for beauty products |
| **Order fulfillment metrics** | Month 2 | Must-have | <24h processing, >95% accuracy |
| **Customer satisfaction scores** | Month 3 | Must-have | NPS >50, CSAT >4.5/5 |

**Beauty industry benchmarks:**
- Gross margins: 70-85% for premium beauty
- CAC payback period: 3-6 months
- Repeat purchase rate: 40-60% within 6 months

## 💡 Key Success Factors

1. **Personal Finance Discipline**: Absolute separation is non-negotiable for investor confidence
2. **Metrics-Driven Growth**: Unit economics must be rock-solid before Series A
3. **Regulatory Compliance**: Singapore/ASEAN beauty regulations are complex but manageable
4. **Team Building**: Right hires at right time to demonstrate scalability
5. **Brand Protection**: IP strategy is crucial in beauty industry
6. **Investor Relationships**: Start building relationships 6+ months before needing money
