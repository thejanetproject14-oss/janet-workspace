## Product Roadmap: What to Launch and When

### Current Revenue Pillars (Mar 2026)
1. **Lip Care** -- Tinted Lip Ganache ($30) ← hero product
2. **Fragrance** -- 5 perfumes ($43-$180) ← highest ticket
3. **Hair Care** -- Sunday Rituals Hair Oil ($35)
4. **Accessories** -- charms, bags, pouches, pins ($9-$138)

### New Product Launches

#### H1 2026 (Apr-Jun): Lip Ecosystem Expansion
- **Satin Lip Liner (x5 shades to match ganache)** -- $18-22
  - Why: Lip liner + ganache bundle = $48-52 (AOV boost), creates a "system"
  - COGS est: $3-5/unit (OEM lip liner is cheap)
  - Margin: ~78%
  - **Bundle: "The Sweet Pair" -- liner + ganache = $45 (vs $48-52 separate)** → drives AOV
  - MOQ: 200-500 units per shade

#### H2 2026 (Jul-Dec): Category Deepening
- **Lip Gloss / Lip Oil (x3-5 shades)** -- $24-28
  - Why: Lower price point = trial product, brings new customers into the lip ecosystem
  - Pairs with ganache (gloss over ganache = viral content moment)
  - COGS est: $3-5/unit
  - Margin: ~82%

- **Hair Oil Minis (30ml travel size)** -- $15-18
  - Why: Trial size reduces risk for new customers, gift-able, travel-friendly
  - COGS est: $3-4/unit
  - Margin: ~78%

- **Discovery Perfume Set (5 x 2ml samples)** -- $25-30
  - Why: Perfume is high-ticket but high-risk for blind buys. Sampler → converts to full bottle
  - COGS est: $5-7/set
  - Margin: ~77%

#### H1 2027: Scale Products
- **Lip Care Set (liner + ganache + gloss)** -- $65-70
  - Full system play. Gift-able. High AOV.

- **Body Oil / Body Mist** -- $28-38
  - Natural extension of fragrance + hair oil positioning
  - Leverages existing perfume supply chain
  - COGS est: $5-8/unit

- **Limited Edition Seasonal Drops** (every quarter)
  - 1-2 exclusive shades or scents
  - Creates urgency + collectibility
  - Drives repeat traffic

#### H2 2027: Authority Products
- **Skincare entry** (lip mask or lip scrub) -- $20-25
  - Stays in the lip ecosystem
  - Adds "treatment" to the routine

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Content is the engine.** At $1,500/mo budget, organic reach is everything. 1 reel/day minimum for 12 months straight.
2. **Lip ganache must go viral at least once.** One TikTok hit = months of revenue. Optimize for shareability (dessert themes, ASMR, swatches on different skin tones).
3. **AOV > $50 is non-negotiable.** Bundles, cross-sells, free shipping thresholds ($50+). Every dollar of AOV compounds.
4. **Repeat rate is the multiplier.** Email flows (post-purchase, browse abandon, win-back) drive this. Set up Klaviyo by Month 2.
5. **Don't over-SKU.** Launch new products only when current ones are selling consistently. Each SKU = inventory risk.
6. **Korea trip in May is a sourcing goldmine.** Lock in OEM relationships for lip liner + lip gloss at COSMOBEAUTY.
7. **Pop-ups are profit AND marketing.** Every night market appearance is both cash and content.
