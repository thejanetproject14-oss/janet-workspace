## Concept
Intimate influencer brunch. Editorial, aesthetic, curated. Not a party -- a moment.
Vibe: ritual luxury meets golden hour. Think: Jaipur pinks, fresh botanicals, warm textures, natural light.

## Format
- **Type:** Seated brunch, curated guest list
- **Size:** 10-15 guests (micro-influencers + a few friends/creatives)
- **Duration:** ~2.5 hours
- **Target date:** Mid-September 2025 (Saturday or Sunday, 11am-1:30pm)

## Venue Criteria
- Natural light (large windows or outdoor area)
- Aesthetic interior (not generic)
- Seats 15 comfortably
- Willing to do private/semi-private booking
- Good brunch menu with vegetarian options
- Ideally: central Singapore (Tiong Bahru, Dempsey, Tanjong Pagar, Joo Chiat)

## PR Gift Bag Contents (~$125 retail value)
1. Lip product (hero SKU)
2. Bag charm or accessory
3. Makeup pouch or silk scrunchie
4. Tarnish-free charm (stainless steel PVD)
5. Brand card with QR to website
6. Handwritten note (personal touch -- Janani writes each)

**Packaging:** On-brand. Tissue paper, branded sticker, maybe a small box or reusable pouch. Not wasteful, not cheap.
