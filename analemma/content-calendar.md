## üß† Content Do's and Don'ts

### DO ‚úÖ
- Show the real, messy process ‚Äî perfection is boring, relatability builds trust
- Respond to every comment and DM during pre-launch (you're small, this is your superpower)
- Use the "save this post" CTA ‚Äî saves are the #1 algorithm signal on Instagram in 2026
- Share your genuine reactions ‚Äî first time holding the final product, first order, etc.
- Repurpose everything ‚Äî a Reel becomes stories becomes a TikTok becomes a still for a post
- Use close-up, textural shots ‚Äî ganache texture is inherently satisfying content

### DON'T ‚ùå
- Don't use AI-generated video ‚Äî it clashes with the "intentional" brand pillar
- Don't post and ghost ‚Äî engage for 15-30 min after every post (comment back, like replies)
- Don't reveal everything in Week 1 ‚Äî the tease-reveal-launch arc needs tension
- Don't apologize for selling ‚Äî you made something incredible, promote it proudly
- Don't compare yourself to big brands ‚Äî your story is your edge
- Don't buy followers before launch ‚Äî fake engagement will tank your reach when it matters most
