🍒 The Little Things

Analemma started with a simple belief: the small, beautiful objects you carry with you every day should feel like they were made for you. Not mass-produced. Not boring. Not something you settle for because it was on sale.

We're talking about the bag charm that catches a stranger's eye on the MRT. The lip colour that smells like the dessert it's named after. The makeup pouch that's almost too pretty to put inside your bag. Almost.

These are the little things that turn a regular Tuesday into something worth noticing.

🎨 Colour is the Point

We don't do beige. We don't do "goes with everything." We do sunset orange, deep cherry red, forest green, electric magenta, berry blue, and Jaipur pink. We do colour that makes you feel something.

Every product in the Analemma world has a personality. A name. A reason for being that specific shade. Our fruit bag charms aren't just cute (they are very cute) -- they're little declarations of who you are today.

Feeling bold? Clip on Cherry on Top. Feeling bright? Squeeze the Day is right there. Feeling quietly beautiful? Feeling Blue-tiful was made for that exact mood.

🧁 Beauty That Smells Like Dessert

Our Sweet Treat lip ganache collection is five shades of creamy, buildable lip colour -- and each one is scented to match its name. Raspberry Coulis smells like raspberries. Hot Cocoa smells like cocoa. Tiramisu smells like coffee and cream.

We did this on purpose. Because putting on lip colour should feel like a tiny treat, not a chore. One swipe and your whole morning gets a little sweeter.

And yes, everything is 100% vegan. Every formula, every material, every brush bristle. No exceptions.

✨ Collect, Personalise, Make It Yours

Here's what we love most: watching people mix and match. A blueberry charm next to a lavender enamel pin. A magenta pouch peeking out of a dark forest green bag. Maraschino Cherry lips with a gold charm clipped to the zipper.

There's no "correct" combination. There's just yours.

We design pieces that play well together but don't demand coordination. Pick what catches your eye. Clip it on. Pin it. Carry it. Layer your world with colour and see what happens.

(What happens is you'll feel great. We've tested this.)

🌸 Why "Analemma"?

An analemma is the figure-eight pattern the sun traces across the sky over the course of a year. It's a symbol of cycles, of return, of the beautiful predictability of showing up -- day after day -- and still finding something new in the light.

We chose the name because beauty, to us, isn't about transformation. It's about ritual. The daily return to yourself. The small, intentional acts of choosing colour, choosing care, choosing joy.

Every time you reach for that lip ganache or clip on that bag charm, you're doing something quietly powerful: you're deciding that today matters enough to make it beautiful.
