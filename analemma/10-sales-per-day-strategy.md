# Analemma: 10 Sales/Day in 90 Days

**Target:** 10 orders/day consistently by ~June 1, 2026
**Starting point:** Pre-launch (website goes live Mar 15)
**Current IG:** ~80-950 followers (low base)
**AOV assumption:** $30-45 (lip ganache is hero at $30)
**Revenue at target:** $300-450/day = $9,000-13,500/month

---

## The Math

10 sales/day from a Shopify store requires roughly:
- **2% conversion rate** (industry standard for beauty) = **500 unique visitors/day**
- **3% conversion rate** (good, with strong brand + UGC) = **333 visitors/day**

So the real question is: **how do you get 333-500 daily visitors who are predisposed to buy?**

There are only 4 traffic sources that matter at this stage:
1. **Organic social** (Instagram/TikTok -- free but requires content volume)
2. **Influencer/UGC seeding** (semi-free, product cost only)
3. **Paid ads** (Meta/TikTok -- costs money but scales)
4. **Offline-to-online** (pop-ups, events, offline store -- drives both direct sales + online repeat)

You can't do paid ads at scale right now (no budget). So the 90-day plan leans heavily on 1, 2, and 4.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4 / Mar 15 - Apr 12)

**Goal:** First 50 customers. Build the proof engine.

### Content Machine (non-negotiable)
- **Post 2x/day on Instagram.** One feed post (carousel or reel), one Story. Every single day.
- **Reels > carousels > static.** Instagram's algorithm rewards video. 15-30 second reels of lip ganache application, texture close-ups, ASMR opening.
- **TikTok mirror.** Repost every reel to TikTok. Different algorithm, different audience. Free reach.
- **Content pillars:**
  - Product (swatches, texture, ASMR, application)
  - Lifestyle (beauty routines, "get ready with me," pouch/bag styling)
  - Education (what makes a lip ganache different, vegan beauty, Ayurvedic ingredients in hair oil)
  - Personality (founder story, behind the brand, day-in-life building Analemma)

### Influencer Seeding (your highest-ROI move)
- **Send 30-50 PR boxes** in the first 2 weeks post-launch
- Target: Singapore-based beauty/lifestyle micro-influencers (1K-30K followers)
- **Don't ask for posts.** Just send gorgeous PR boxes with a handwritten note. The product + packaging does the talking.
- Conversion expectation: 40-60% will post something. That's 12-30 organic posts from real people.
- **Where to find them:** Instagram hashtags (#sgbeauty, #sgmakeup, #beautybloggersg), TikTok search, Telegram beauty groups
- Cost: ~$150-250 in product cost (lip ganache + charm + pouch per box = ~$13 product cost per box)
- **This is the single highest ROI thing you can do in month 1.**

### Email List
- Set up Klaviyo (free up to 250 contacts)
- Pop-up on site: "Get 10% off your first order" -- captures emails
- Launch day email blast to anyone who's shown interest
- Post-purchase flow: thank you + review request + cross-sell

### First Event
- **Brunch launch** (already planned for Bridgerton -- build on this)
- Invite 15-20 people. Friends, acquaintances, anyone with a following.
- Every attendee leaves with product in hand + has already tried it on
- Capture content at the event. Lots of it.

**Week 4 target:** 50 orders, 200+ email subscribers, 15+ UGC posts from influencers

## Phase 2: Amplify (Weeks 5-8 / Apr 13 - May 10)

**Goal:** Scale to 3-5 sales/day. Build repeat + referral loops.

### Content Acceleration
- Increase to **3 posts/day** if capacity allows (2 reels + 1 carousel)
- Start **"Review" content** -- repost UGC from influencers and early buyers
- **Collaborate feature** on Instagram -- co-post with influencers who tagged you (doubles reach)
- Launch **"Lip Ganache of the Week"** series -- spotlight one shade, create urgency

### Referral Program
- "Give $5, Get $5" -- simple referral code per customer
- Include referral card in every order's packaging
- WhatsApp/Telegram share link for easy forwarding

### Strategic Bundles (increase AOV)
- **The Sweet Treat Set:** Any 2 lip ganaches + metallic charm = $72 (save $10)
- **The Full Collection:** All 5 shades + charm = $155 (save $17)
- **The Carry Your World Kit:** Pouch + 2 lip ganaches + charm = $108 (save $10)
- Bundles increase AOV from $30 to $72-155. Fewer customers needed per day.

### Micro-Pop-ups (1-2 per month)
- Weekend pop-ups at beauty/lifestyle events
- Target: ARTBOX, maker markets, beauty fairs
- Not about revenue -- about email capture + product trial + content
- Every pop-up = 20-30 new emails + photos/videos of real people trying products

### Paid UGC (small budget)
- Pay 3-5 micro-influencers $50-100 each for dedicated review content
- You own the content -- use in ads later
- Total spend: $150-500/month
- **Don't run paid ads yet.** Just collect content assets.

**Week 8 target:** 3-5 orders/day, 500+ email subscribers, 500+ IG followers, 40+ UGC assets

## Phase 3: Scale (Weeks 9-12 / May 11 - Jun 7)

**Goal:** Hit 10 sales/day consistently.

### Paid Ads (Meta -- start small)
- By now you have: 40+ UGC assets, customer reviews, social proof
- Start with $15-20/day on Meta (Instagram + Facebook)
- **Only run ads with UGC content** -- not branded graphics. Real people using real products.
- Target: Singapore women 18-35, interests in K-beauty, vegan beauty, lip products
- Retarget: website visitors, IG engagers, email list lookalikes
- At $15/day with $10-15 CPA, that's 1-2 extra orders/day from ads alone
- Scale to $30-50/day as ROAS proves out (target 3x+ ROAS)

### Email Marketing (revenue driver)
- By now your list should be 500-1000+
- Weekly email: new shade spotlight, limited drops, bundle deals
- Abandoned cart recovery (automated -- Klaviyo does this free)
- **Email should drive 20-30% of revenue** by this point

### Community Building
- Launch a Telegram channel or WhatsApp broadcast for "Analemma Insiders"
- Early access to new shades, exclusive bundles, BTS content
- This becomes your owned audience -- not dependent on algorithm

### Second Wave Seeding
- Send 20-30 more PR boxes, this time targeting slightly larger accounts (10K-50K)
- Include regional targets: Malaysia, Indonesia (shipping cost is low, market is massive)
- One viral TikTok from a 30K account = hundreds of sales

**Week 12 target:** 10 orders/day, $8K+/month revenue, 1000+ email list, 1500+ IG

## Offline Store Analysis: Singapore

### Why Offline Matters for Analemma
Beauty is tactile. People want to swatch, smell, feel. Lip ganache especially -- the texture IS the differentiator. An offline presence converts skeptics into buyers and drives online repeat purchases.

### But: You Don't Need a Full Store Right Now.
What you need is **offline presence** -- which can be:
1. Consignment in existing stores
2. Pop-up spaces (weekend/monthly)
3. Shared retail (co-op booth)
4. Eventually: own micro-store or kiosk

### Best Areas in Singapore (ranked by fit for Analemma)

**1. Haji Lane / Arab Street (Kampong Glam)**
- **Why:** Indie brand paradise. Young, aesthetic-seeking crowd. Tourists + locals. The vibe IS Analemma -- colourful, curated, intentional.
- **Footfall:** High on weekends, moderate weekdays. Strong IG-worthy foot traffic.
- **Rent:** $3,000-6,000/month for a small shophouse unit
- **Best for:** First permanent space. Consignment in existing boutiques is possible here too.
- **Downside:** Weekday foot traffic drops.
- ⭐ **My #1 recommendation for Analemma's first offline space.**

**2. Orchard Road (Mandarin Gallery / Design Orchard)**
- **Why:** Highest footfall in Singapore. Design Orchard specifically champions local brands.
- **Footfall:** Massive. 100K+ daily across Orchard belt.
- **Rent:** Design Orchard pop-up: ~$1,500-3,000/month for a booth. Full retail on Orchard: $8,000-20,000+ (too expensive).
- **Best for:** Pop-ups, Design Orchard consignment booth
- **Downside:** Full retail is cost-prohibitive. Competition with massive beauty brands.

**3. Funan Mall**
- **Why:** Tech-meets-lifestyle crowd. High disposable income. Good mix of locals + tourists.
- **Footfall:** Strong. Attracts 20-35 demographic.
- **Rent:** $4,000-8,000/month for small retail. Pop-up spaces available.
- **Best for:** Pop-up or kiosk. The crowd appreciates "new" and "different."

**4. Tiong Bahru**
- **Why:** Hipster crowd, high spending power, appreciates craft/indie brands.
- **Footfall:** Moderate but highly targeted. Quality over quantity.
- **Rent:** $3,000-5,000/month
- **Best for:** If your buyer persona is "discerning, aesthetic-conscious, willing to pay for quality" -- this is your crowd.

**5. Bugis / Bugis+**
- **Why:** Young crowd, high footfall, beauty-oriented shoppers.
- **Footfall:** Very high. One of the busiest areas outside Orchard.
- **Rent:** $3,500-7,000/month
- **Best for:** Younger demographic. Good for accessories + lip products.

**6. Jewel Changi / Terminal areas**
- **Why:** Captive audience (travelers), high impulse purchase rate, premium positioning.
- **Footfall:** Very high
- **Rent:** $6,000-15,000+ (expensive but high conversion)
- **Best for:** Eventually. Not for first store.

### My Recommendation (Right Now, No Budget):

**Immediate (free/low-cost):**
1. **Design Orchard consignment** -- apply for a shelf/booth. They actively seek local beauty brands. Costs are low, exposure is high.
2. **Naiise / local boutique consignment** -- place products in existing stores for a commission split (typically 30-40% to store)
3. **Weekend pop-ups** at ARTBOX, The Local People markets, Maker markets -- $100-300/booth

**When you have $3-5K/month for rent:**
1. **Haji Lane micro-store or shared space** -- find a co-retail partner (another indie brand) and split a shophouse unit

**When you hit $15K+/month revenue:**
1. **Design Orchard dedicated booth** or **Funan kiosk**

## Key Risks

1. **Content bottleneck.** 2 posts/day is a lot for one person. Batch shoot on weekends (shoot 10-14 pieces, schedule through the week). Consider a part-time content assistant.
2. **Conversion rate below 2%.** If the website doesn't convert, more traffic won't help. Monitor closely and optimize product pages, checkout flow, reviews.
3. **No organic traction.** If IG/TikTok don't grow, you'll need paid ads sooner. Have $500 reserved for this.
4. **Over-diversification.** Your catalog is wide (lip ganache, hair oil, bags, charms, pouches, pins, perfumes). For this 90-day sprint: **lead with lip ganache only.** Everything else is cross-sell/upsell. One hero. One message.
