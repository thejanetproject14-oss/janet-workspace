## 1. Rhode
**URL:** rhodeskin.com | **Platform:** Shopify
**Positioning:** Luxury minimalist skincare by Hailey Bieber. Clean, glazed-skin aesthetic.

### Homepage Layout
- **Hero:** Full-bleed announcement banner ("Caffeine Reset + Peptide Lip Boost have arrived") with swipeable product cards beneath
- **Structure:** Horizontally scrollable product grid ‚Äî every product visible on homepage as individual cards with single action words (boost, sculpt, edit, bag, seal, depuff, prep)
- **Bottom sections:** Mission statement, philanthropy, sustainability ‚Äî each as a single elegant sentence
- **Footer:** Minimal, clean

### Product Page Structure
- **Standout feature:** Incredibly detailed product pages ‚Äî arguably best-in-class
- Product hero with key claims and price
- "Good for / Feels like / Smells like / FYI" quick reference box (e.g., "Smells like: A fresh strawberry glazed donut")
- Interactive ingredient list ‚Äî click any ingredient to learn about it (powered by ClearForMe)
- Consumer study results with percentage stats (e.g., "100% agreed it reduces the appearance of dry lips")
- Full skincare routine integration showing where the product fits in a 5-step routine
- Detailed recycling/packaging section with material breakdown
- "What's Inside" deep-dive on hero ingredient

### Copy Style
- **Ultra-minimal, lowercase** ‚Äî brand name and product names always lowercase ("rhode," "peptide lip treatment," "barrier restore cream")
- Single-word descriptors as section headers: "boost," "sculpt," "seal," "depuff," "prep," "glaze," "nourish"
- Tagline formula: product name + brief description (e.g., "peptide glazing fluid ‚Äî Dewy gel serum")
- Sensory language: "Nourishing GLAZE that HYDRATES + replenishes"
- Selective use of ALL CAPS for emphasis

### Photography Approach
- Clean product shots on muted/soft backgrounds
- Strong emphasis on texture and product-on-skin shots
- Hailey Bieber herself as face of brand (founder-forward, aspirational)
- Glazed, dewy aesthetic throughout ‚Äî consistent light/bright mood

### Navigation
- Minimal nav bar with logo center
- Products organized by function, not category
- Product cards on homepage serve as the primary navigation

### Checkout Flow
- Direct "Add to Cart" from product cards on homepage
- Streamlined Shopify checkout

### Brand Story Placement
- Bottom of homepage: mission, philanthropy, sustainability ‚Äî three concise sections
- No long "About" page narrative ‚Äî lets the product speak

### Color Palette
- **Warm neutrals:** cream, off-white, soft beige
- **Accents:** Soft pinks, warm earth tones from product colors
- Very restrained ‚Äî white space is the dominant design element

### Typography
- **All lowercase custom sans-serif** ‚Äî distinctive and on-brand
- Clean, readable, modern
- Large type for product names, small for descriptions

### What Works Well
- ‚úÖ Food/dessert naming convention (Strawberry Glaze, Toast) ‚Äî DIRECTLY relevant to Analemma
- ‚úÖ "Smells like" descriptor on every product ‚Äî sensory selling
- ‚úÖ Ingredient transparency without feeling clinical
- ‚úÖ Lowercase branding creates intimacy and modernity
- ‚úÖ Consumer study data builds trust
- ‚úÖ Routine integration helps upsell

### What to Avoid
- ‚ö†Ô∏è The minimalism only works because of massive brand awareness (Hailey Bieber) ‚Äî a smaller brand may need more narrative
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Very little educational content ‚Äî assumes customer already knows the brand
