## Product Roadmap (Sunshine's Vision)

### H1 2026 (Mar-Jun): LAUNCH
**Hero products: Lip Ganache + Perfume**
- Lip Ganache x5 shades @ $30 -- hero, volume driver
- Perfume 15ml x5 @ $42 -- mid-ticket, brand elevation
- Perfume 100ml x5 @ $180 -- premium, high-margin anchor
- Hair Oil @ $35 -- consistent seller
- Accessories -- impulse buys, gifting, AOV boosters

**Key bundles to build AOV:**
- "Sweet Treat Duo" (2x ganache) @ $55 (vs $60 separate) → save $5
- "Ganache + Charm" @ $48 (vs $52)
- "Perfume + Ganache" @ $65 (vs $72)
- Free shipping threshold: $50

### H2 2026 (Jul-Dec): LIP ECOSYSTEM + SKIN-HYBRID
**New launches: Satin Lip Liner + Hyaluronic Squalane Blush Balm**

#### Satin Lip Liner (x5 shades matching ganache)
- **Price:** $20-24
- **Est. COGS:** $3-5/unit
- **Margin:** ~79-83%
- **Why it works:**
  - Completes the lip routine: liner → ganache = system sale
  - Drives bundles: "The Sweet Pair" (liner + ganache) @ $45-48
  - Lip liner is the most repurchased lip product (sharpening = usage = repeat)
  - Low COGS, high perceived value
  - Content goldmine: application tutorials, shade matching

#### Hyaluronic Squalane Blush Balm
- **Price:** $28-34
- **Est. COGS:** $5-8/unit (skincare-grade actives cost more)
- **Margin:** ~74-82%
- **Why this is brilliant:**
  - Bridges skincare + colour = "skinimalism" trend, massive growth category
  - Hyaluronic acid + squalane = hydration + barrier repair while wearing colour
  - Balm format = low waste, portable, refillable potential
  - Plays to the Analemma "ritual beauty" positioning -- it's not just makeup, it's care
  - Pairs with ganache for a "no-makeup makeup" full-face routine
  - Multi-use appeal: cheeks, lips, even eyelids = content versatility
  - Opens the "face" category without committing to full skincare
- **Shades:** 4-6 (recommend starting with 4: a nude-pink, warm peach, berry, and a bronzy rose)
- **Naming convention:** Keep the food/botanical language -- "Peach Sorbet," "Rose Petal," "Cinnamon Sugar," "Berry Compote"
- **MOQ estimate:** 300-500 units per shade

**H2 bundles:**
- "The Full Ritual" (liner + ganache + blush balm) @ $68-72
- "Everyday Glow" (ganache + blush balm) @ $52-55
- "Lip Lover Set" (liner + ganache) @ $45-48

### H1 2027 (Jan-Jun): EYE CATEGORY + SCALE
**New launch: Magnetic Eyelashes**

#### Magnetic Eyelashes
- **Price:** $18-28 (depending on tier -- natural vs dramatic)
- **Est. COGS:** $3-6/unit
- **Margin:** ~78-83%
- **Why it works:**
  - Vegan-friendly (no mink -- aligns with brand values)
  - Reusable = sustainability angle = brand alignment
  - High repeat purchase (adhesive strips, wear-and-tear replacement)
  - Opens the "eye" category without going into eyeshadow complexity
  - Extremely content-friendly: before/after, application tutorials, "3 looks 1 lash"
  - Lower price point = trial-friendly
  - Can sell a "magnetic liner" as companion product later
- **Styles:** 3-5 (natural everyday, wispy, dramatic, cat-eye)
- **Bundle potential:**
  - "Full Face Kit" (lashes + ganache + blush balm) @ $65-75
  - "Eye + Lip" (lashes + ganache) @ $42-48

### H2 2027 (Jul-Dec): DEEPEN + SEASONAL

**Expand existing lines rather than new categories:**
- Blush balm: add 2-3 new seasonal shades
- Lip liner: limited edition metallic/shimmer shades
- Magnetic lashes: add magnetic eyeliner as companion

- Hair oil minis (30ml travel) @ $15-18 -- trial + gifting

## Critical Success Factors

1. **System selling is your superpower.** Liner + ganache + blush balm = a 3-step ritual. That's $72-86 per face. Market the system, not individual products.
2. **Blush balm is the sleeper hit.** Skincare-hybrid colour is the fastest growing segment in beauty. Position it as "skincare that happens to be beautiful."
3. **Content is the engine.** 1 reel/day for 24 months. Non-negotiable at $1,500/mo.
4. **Korea trip = sourcing everything.** Lock lip liner + lash OEM relationships at COSMOBEAUTY. Potentially blush balm formulator too.
5. **AOV > $50 via bundles.** Every product launch should come with 2-3 bundle options.
6. **Perfume is the margin king.** Push the 100ml conversion. Discovery set in H2 2027 unlocks trial.
7. **Pop-ups = cash + content + customer data.** Track who buys what for email segmentation.
8. **Don't launch more than 2 new products per half.** Each launch needs full content support.
