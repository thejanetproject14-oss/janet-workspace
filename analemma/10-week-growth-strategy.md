## Discovery Strategy

### Reels-First Approach
- Post 2-3 Reels/week minimum (these are your growth engine)
- First 3 seconds = hook (text overlay or visual surprise)
- 15-30 seconds optimal for discovery
- Use trending audio when it fits the aesthetic (check Reels tab daily)
- Always add captions/subtitles
- Cross-post every Reel to TikTok (same day or next day)

### TikTok Cross-Posting
- Repost every Instagram Reel to TikTok (without the IG watermark -- save before posting to IG, or use SnapTik)
- Add native TikTok captions and trending sounds
- TikTok favors raw/authentic > polished -- lean into that
- Post at 7-9pm SGT on TikTok (peak Singapore hours)
- Use TikTok-native language in captions ("no bc why does this lip product smell like ACTUAL tiramisu üò≠")

### Engagement Tactics (15-20 min/day)
- **Before posting:** Spend 10 min engaging with accounts in your niche (comment genuinely on 10-15 posts)
- **After posting:** Spend 10 min replying to every comment within the first hour (algorithm boost)
- **Daily:** Follow 5-10 relevant accounts (beauty bloggers, vegan brands, SG food accounts)
- **Comment strategy:** Leave thoughtful comments (not "nice!") on accounts with 1K-50K followers -- their audiences will see you
- **Stories engagement:** React to other brands' stories, respond to polls, use "Add Yours" stickers

### Collaboration Opportunities
- **Dessert/caf√© collab:** Partner with a SG caf√© for a "Sweet Treat x [caf√©]" content shoot -- you bring the lip products, they get exposure
- **Cross-features:** Ask nano-influencers (500-5K) for story takeovers or joint content
- **Beauty community:** Join SG beauty Telegram/WhatsApp groups, participate genuinely
- **Bridgerton party (Feb 28):** Every guest = potential content creator. Set up a photo station with products.

## PR & Influencer Strategy

### Pre-Send (Feb 19-28)
- DM influencers by Feb 25 (warm intro, no hard pitch)
- Message template: personal, reference their content, explain why you chose them
- Don't say "we'd love to send you products in exchange for a post"
- DO say "we made something we think you'd genuinely love -- can we send you a box?"
- Collect addresses from confirmed influencers

### Send Day (Mar 1)
- 25 PR boxes shipped
- Each box includes personal handwritten note or printed brand card
- Include a small card with IG handle + hashtag suggestion (not mandatory)
- Text "your box is on its way! üíõ" with a photo of the packed box

### Post-Send (Mar 2-15)
- **Day 2-3:** "Has your box arrived?" check-in (casual, not pushy)
- **Day 5-7:** If they posted -- repost to stories with genuine thank you
- **Day 7-10:** If they haven't posted -- that's OK. Send a gentle "hope you're enjoying it! would love to know your fave flavor üç´" 
- **Day 14:** Final soft touch -- "any favorites?" No pressure.
- **Never:** Ask why they didn't post. Never guilt trip.

### Repurposing Influencer Content
- Repost every influencer story/post to your stories (with credit)
- Screenshot and save for "As Seen On" highlights
- Create a carousel: "What SG creators are saying about Sweet Treat"
- With permission, use their photos/videos in your feed posts
- Save all UGC in a folder for website testimonials
