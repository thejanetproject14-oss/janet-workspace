## Risk Factors & Contingencies

### High-Risk Scenarios
1. **Cash Flow Constraint**
   - *Risk:* Credit limits restrict ad spend scaling
   - *Mitigation:* Focus organic growth, secure additional funding
   - *Pivot:* Extend timeline, lower daily sales target to 6/day

2. **iOS Privacy Changes**
   - *Risk:* Facebook tracking becomes less effective
   - *Mitigation:* Diversify to TikTok, Google, email marketing
   - *Pivot:* Increase organic content investment by 40%

3. **Competition Increase**
   - *Risk:* Large brands enter Ayurvedic beauty space
   - *Mitigation:* Build strong brand loyalty, unique formulations
   - *Pivot:* Focus on niche markets (Singapore, SEA)

### Medium-Risk Scenarios
4. **Supply Chain Issues**
   - *Risk:* Korea sourcing delays affect product launches
   - *Mitigation:* Dual supplier relationships, local alternatives
   - *Pivot:* Focus on existing product optimization

5. **Conversion Rate Plateau**
   - *Risk:* Unable to improve from 1.5% to 2.0%
   - *Mitigation:* Intensive A/B testing, UX improvements
   - *Pivot:* Increase traffic targets proportionally

### Success Accelerators
- **Viral TikTok Content:** Could accelerate timeline by 2 months
- **Celebrity Endorsement:** Organic reach boost
- **Product Award/Recognition:** Instant credibility boost
- **Successful Korea Partnerships:** Unique product advantages
