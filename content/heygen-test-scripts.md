## Script 2: "Why Peptides in a Lip Product?"
**Duration:** ~40 sec
**Tone:** Smart, conversational

Ok quick ingredient spotlight. Our Lip Ganache has peptides in it. And you might be thinking, peptides? In a lip product? That is usually in serums and eye creams.

Exactly. Your lips are skin too. Actually they are some of the thinnest skin on your body. Peptides signal your skin to produce more collagen, which means fuller, plumper lips over time. Not the filler kind. The your-lips-are-just-healthier kind.

Combined with hyaluronic acid for hydration and plant squalane to lock it all in, you are basically giving your lips a facial every time you swipe this on.

Skincare for your lips. That is the whole point.

## Script 4: "3 Things You Didn't Know About Your Lip Balm"
**Duration:** ~40 sec
**Tone:** Punchy, myth-busting

Three things your lip balm is probably not telling you.

One. Most lip balms contain ingredients that actually dry your lips out over time. Menthol, camphor, phenol. They feel nice for five minutes, then your lips need more. That is not hydration. That is dependency.

Two. SPF lip balms almost never have enough coverage. You would need to reapply every twenty minutes to get the protection on the label.

Three. If your lip product does not have occlusive ingredients like shea butter or squalane, the moisture is evaporating right off your lips.

Our Lip Ganache has cocoa butter, shea butter, and plant squalane. It locks hydration in. No menthol. No tricks. Just lips that are actually healthy.
