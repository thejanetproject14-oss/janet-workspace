## 🧍 DAY-OF: BOOTH OPERATOR GUIDE

**Your Setup (personal):**
- [ ] Water bottle (you'll forget to hydrate)
- [ ] Snacks (you get hungry ~1.5 hours in)
- [ ] Phone charger + portable battery
- [ ] Comfortable shoes (standing all day)
- [ ] Light jacket (aircon venues can be cold)

**Booth Energy:**
- Stand beside or in front of the table, not behind it
- Opening lines ready:
  - "Want to try our Lip Ganache?" (product-first)
  - "Pull a free tarot card!" (activity-first, lower barrier)
  - "Everything here is handmade -- want to take a look?" (story-first)
- Know your 10-second pitch:
  - "Analemma is about the little things that bring joy. Our hero product is Lip Ganache -- it's a lip balm that feels like dessert. Want to try a shade?"
- If someone's browsing but not engaging, let them be for 10 seconds, then softly offer: "Happy to answer any questions!"
- DON'T hard sell. Let the products + display + tarot pull do the work.

**Sales Tips:**
- Lead with testers -- once it's on their lips, it sells itself
- Mention the enamel pin giveaway after they've browsed (reward, not bribe)
- If someone buys multiple items, thank them genuinely
- Ask every buyer: "Can I get your Instagram? We'd love to feature you!"
- Track what sells best -- note it down. This data is gold for future events.

- [ ] Candid shots of people browsing / trying products
- [ ] Stories throughout the day (BTS, crowd, product close-ups)
