# Founder Story -- Janani G

## The Arc
- Grew up in Bangalore. First beauty memories: Lakme kajal from Sharadha Fancy Stores, Fuso makeup from Mustafa Centre in Singapore.
- Standing in front of a mirror with kajal = first experience of "romanticizing life."
- Apple internship 2014. First paycheck → entire thing spent at Sephora.
- Spent 10 years jumping between ideas (organized chaos with spreadsheets).
- Career: Grab (scaled GrabFood post-Uber acquisition, ~1K to ~1M orders/month, 173% user growth SEA, launched Thailand -- 4M orders in <4 months, $800M revenue). Adyen (launched own acquiring in India, built team 0→45, UPI, settlement, reconciliation).
- Now at terminal 3: identity infrastructure, MetaMask, Mastercard/Visa, agent authorization.
- Beauty was always the thread. The thing she kept coming back to.
- The insight: sensory experiences make you present. Scent = presence. What if a lip product could do that?
- Built Analemma: ingredients-first, scented, dessert-named lip ganache. Fun, colourful, collectible.
