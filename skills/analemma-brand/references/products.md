# Analemma Product Catalog

## Hero: Sweet Treat Lip Ganache Collection

ALL VEGAN. Always "tinted lip ganache" -- never "lip balm" or "lipstick."

### Shades

1. **Raspberry Coulis** -- berry-toned, smells like raspberry
2. **Strawberry Jam** -- pink-red, smells like strawberry
3. **Hot Cocoa** -- warm brown nude, smells like chocolate
4. **Maraschino Cherry** -- deep cherry red, smells like cherry
5. **Tiramisu** -- warm nude-brown, smells like tiramisu

### INCI
Ethylhexyl Palmitate, Octyldodecanol, Polyisobutene, Ethylene/Propylene/Styrene Copolymer, Bis(16-Methylheptadecyl) Malate, Squalane, Hyaluronic Acid, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea Butter) Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter, Tocopherol, Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1, Mica, Mild Flavor.

### Key Ingredients (marketing angles)
- **Peptides** (Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1) -- anti-aging, plumping
- **Plant Squalane** -- lightweight hydration, mimics skin's natural oils
- **Hyaluronic Acid** -- moisture retention, plumping
- **Shea Butter** -- deep nourishment, barrier repair
- **Cocoa Butter** -- rich moisture, antioxidant
- **Vitamin E** (Tocopherol) -- antioxidant protection
- **Jojoba Oil** -- balances, soothes, non-greasy

### Product positioning
- Ingredients-first lip nourishment that also gives colour
- Scented (sensory experience = presence = Analemma's thesis)
- Not a lipstick (too heavy/matte). Not a lip balm (too basic/boring). A ganache.
- The ganache metaphor: rich, layered, indulgent, smooth
